Bold Video ("Bold Video", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website, platform, and services.

## 1. What This Policy Covers

This policy applies to:

- The Bold Video website
- Your Bold Video account
- Videos, transcripts, and data you upload or connect
- Any related tools, features, or communications

## 2. Information We Collect

We only collect what is necessary to provide and improve the service.

### Information you provide

- Name, email address, and account details
- Billing and payment information
- Videos you upload or connect from third-party platforms
- Questions, prompts, or inputs submitted through the platform
- Support messages or communications

### Information collected automatically

- Log data such as IP address, browser type, and usage activity
- Device and session information
- Cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and operate the Bold Video service
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Generate transcripts, search results, and AI-powered responses
- Improve performance, reliability, and user experience
- Communicate with you about your account or service updates
- Maintain security and prevent abuse

We do not sell your data.

## 4. Video Content and Privacy

Your videos and related content are private by default.

- Your content is only accessible to you and people you explicitly grant access to
- Videos and transcripts are not made public unless you choose to publish or share them
- We do not use your private video content to train public AI models
- AI processing is limited to delivering the functionality you request

## 5. Sharing of Information

We only share information when necessary:

- With trusted service providers that help us operate the platform (hosting, payments, analytics)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights, safety, or security of Bold Video and its users

All partners are required to handle data securely and confidentially.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

- Secure hosting and encryption where appropriate
- Access controls and authentication
- Ongoing monitoring for misuse or unauthorized access

No system is 100 percent secure, but protecting your content is a core priority.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as:

- Your account is active, or
- It is needed to provide the service, or
- We are legally required to keep it

You can delete your content or close your account at any time.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent where applicable

You can contact us to exercise these rights.

## 9. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep you signed in
- Understand basic usage patterns
- Improve site performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## 10. Third-Party Services

Bold Video may integrate with third-party platforms such as video hosts or payment providers. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this one.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the website or your account.
